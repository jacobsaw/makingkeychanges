HCPSS MS GT Band

Howard County Middle School Gifted & Talented (GT) Band

Mrs. Reichl & Mr. Spang serve as co-directors of the Howard County Gifted & Talented Middle School Band.

Audition Information:  provided to all HCPSS MS Band Directors on September 21. Students must obtain the registration information from their school band director.

Audition Registration:  forms are due to your school band director by October 7, 2016

Auditions:  Wednesday, November 2, 2016 @ Mayfield Woods Middle School. Students will be scheduled for a specific audition time slot between 4 pm and 8 pm. Students will receive their audition time slot from their school band director by October 26.

Audition Requirements: Same as Maryland All-State Jr. Band Music

Ensemble: Rehearses/Performs throughout January 2017. Review last year's handbook as a guide.
