TESTIMONIALS

ADJUDICATOR   It'll blow your mind.

Ensemble directors often rely on an extra set of ears to enhance the quality of their ensemble's sound. Our consultants are available for services such as adjudication festivals and mock adjudications. Hire an adjudicator.

CLINICIAN See for yourself.

Clinics best serve the needs of music programs when there is a collaborative nature between the ensemble conductor and the guest director. Our consultants supplement daily instruction to what the ensemble conductor has currently been teaching. Clinics include one-on-one instruction time between a musical ensemble and a qualified director in the form of ensemble coaching and/or guest conducting. Schedule a clinic.

CURRICULUM ASSESSOR & DEVELOPER It'll blow your mind.

With constant changes to curriculum and alignment with the CORE Standards, school district stakeholders might question the current validity and relevance of their curriculum. Our consultants provide an outside perspective to review current curriculum and provide a comprehensive improvement plan. In this assessment, our consultants examine national trends in music education that the research, literature and practice reflect, acknowledge the best uses of technology for improving student achievement, recognize the strengths and deficits of the current school district curriculum and map suggestions for changes and improvements. Hire a curriculum specialist.

GUEST CONDUCTOR See for yourself.

Our consultants are available to conduct district-wide, county-wide, regional, or all-state ensembles. Their focus is to unite students from various locations, ethnicities, financial statuses, or levels of talent to build a successful musical family in a short rehearsal time span. Hire a guest conductor.

MENTOR See for yourself.

Novice teachers often lack the support needed to master classroom management techniques, connect with populations of students, plan for collaborative events and trips, and Access a mentor.

PRESENTER See for yourself.

Advocacy support to speak to decision-makers as a way of advocating for your value of music education program. Hire a presenter.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPER See for yourself.

Wishing to make professional development time meaningful for music teachers, school district administrators can work with our consultants to develop ideas that parallel and supplement in-service topics specific for music teachers. The topics are endless, and some ideas include using technology, developing assessments, and building learning communities. Schedule a professional development session.
