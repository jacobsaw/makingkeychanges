SERVICES

ADJUDICATOR

Trust an extra set of musically trained ears to enhance the quality of your ensemble's sound. Invite us to adjudicate at a music festival or mock adjudication.

Schedule an adjudication!

CLINICIAN

Supplement collaborative instruction to your daily teaching. Invite us to offer support to musicians and the conductor at an ensemble rehearsal.

Schedule a clinic!

CURRICULUM SPECIALIST

Validate your curriculum to school district stakeholders and improve student achievement. Invite us to assess your curriculum through examination of national trends and research, alignment with the CORE Standards, and implementation of technology. Invite us to assess and develop curriculum for a program.

Schedule an assessment!

GUEST CONDUCTOR

Unite students from various locations, ethnicities, financial statuses, or levels of talent in order to build a successful musical family in a short rehearsal time span. Invite us to conduct district-wide, county-wide, regional, or all-state ensembles.

Schedule a guest conductor!

MENTOR

Acquire support needed on numerous teaching and learning topics, such as mastering effective classroom management techniques, connecting with students and families, planning for collaborative events and trips, and obtaining administrative support. Invite us to provide mentorship.

Schedule a mentorship!

PRESENTER

Demonstrate advocacy support to decision-makers and influential stakeholders as a way of advocating value for educational programs. Invite us to serve as a keynote speaker or presenter.

Schedule a presentation!

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPER

Prepare meaningful professional development time for educators on endless academic topics, such as building learning communities, developing effective classroom management techniques and routines, enhancing relationships with diverse populations of students, formulating assessments, fostering unity in music ensembles, and using technology to strengthen student achievement. Invite us to develop a session!

Schedule a session!
